I am currently pursuing a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) at the esteemed Central University of Rajasthan (CPU), located in Kota, Rajasthan. My research is dedicated to advancing the field of mathematics, and I'm actively engaged in the exploration of specific mathematical areas, contributing to the academic community. This doctoral pursuit reflects my commitment to in-depth research, problem-solving, and innovation, while also allowing me to develop an expertise that can be applied to complex mathematical challenges.
